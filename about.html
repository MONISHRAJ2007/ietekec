Institution of Electronics & Telecommunication Engineers

The Institution of Electronics and Telecommunication Engineers (IETE) is India's foremost professional body dedicated to the advancement of science and technology of Electronics, Telecommunication, and Information Technology. Established in 1953 with its headquarters in New Delhi, IETE has grown to become the nation's most prestigious technical organization in these fields.

IETE serves a community of over 1,50,000 members comprising engineers, scientists, academics, and technocrats across the country. The institution facilitates the exchange of knowledge, ideas, and innovations through conferences, journals, workshops, and student chapter activities across 800+ chapters nationwide.

Recognized by the Government of India, IETE plays a vital role in shaping India's technological future by bridging the gap between academia and industry, nurturing young talent, and contributing to national development through strategic technical initiatives.

History

IETE Through the Years

A proud legacy of over seven decades of advancing India's electronics and telecom landscape.

1953

IETE Founded in India

The Institution of Electronics and Telecommunication Engineers was established in New Delhi, marking a landmark moment for India's technical community.

1970s

Expansion Across India

IETE expanded its network with centers and chapters across major cities and educational institutions throughout India, growing its membership significantly.

2000s

Digital Era Transformation

IETE embraced the information technology revolution, expanding its scope to include software, IT, and digital communication alongside traditional electronics and telecom.

2010s

Student Forums Established

Thousands of IETE Student Forums were established across engineering colleges in India, bringing professional development opportunities directly to students.

2024

IETE KEC Student Forum Active

The IETE Student Forum at Kongu Engineering College continues to thrive, organizing technical events, workshops, and empowering ECE students for the future.
