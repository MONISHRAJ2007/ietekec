Department Overview

Excellence in Electronics & Communication

The Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering at Kongu Engineering College has been at the forefront of technical education since its inception, nurturing future engineers with cutting-edge curriculum and state-of-the-art laboratories.

Our department is committed to producing technically competent, ethically grounded, and industry-ready graduates. With a balanced focus on core fundamentals and emerging technologies, students are equipped to excel in both academia and industry.

The department offers undergraduate (B.E.), postgraduate (M.E.), and doctoral (Ph.D.) programs, attracting the best minds across Tamil Nadu and beyond. Our research culture fosters innovation and problem-solving at the highest levels.

Infrastructure

State-of-the-Art Laboratories

Our well-equipped laboratories provide students with hands-on experience across all major areas of electronics and communication engineering.

🔬

VLSI Design Lab

Equipped with advanced EDA tools for Very Large Scale Integration design, simulation, and FPGA implementation. Industry-standard CAD software for chip design.

📡

Communication Lab

Comprehensive setup for analog and digital communication experiments including modulation techniques, signal transmission, and receiver design with modern test equipment.

💻

Embedded Systems Lab

Hands-on learning with microcontrollers, ARM processors, Raspberry Pi, Arduino, and real-time operating systems for embedded application development.

🎛️

Microprocessor Lab

Study of 8085, 8086 microprocessors and 8051 microcontrollers with programming, interfacing, and peripheral hardware experiments for system-level understanding.

📻

Signal Processing Lab

MATLAB-equipped facility for digital signal and image processing experiments. Advanced DSP kit-based hardware experiments and simulation for real-world applications.

🤖

IoT & Robotics Lab

Cutting-edge lab for Internet of Things, robotics, AI integration, and autonomous systems — preparing students for Industry 4.0 and smart technology development.

Research

Research Areas

Faculty and students collaborate on impactful research across four key domains, driving innovation and publishing in top international journals.

🔌

VLSI & Embedded Systems

Research in low-power VLSI design, FPGA prototyping, ASIC design methodologies, embedded AI, and real-time control systems for automotive and consumer electronics applications.

Low-Power VLSI FPGA Design Embedded AI
📶

Wireless Communication

Focus on 5G/6G communication systems, antenna design, MIMO technology, cognitive radio networks, and spectrum management for next-generation mobile and satellite communication.

5G/6G Networks Antenna Design MIMO
🖼️

Signal & Image Processing

Advanced research in speech processing, biomedical signal analysis, computer vision, deep learning for image recognition, and real-time video analytics for smart surveillance systems.

Computer Vision Biomedical Signals Deep Learning
🌐

IoT & AI Applications

Research in smart home automation, industrial IoT, edge computing, machine learning models for sensor data, autonomous vehicles, and AI-driven energy management systems.

Smart IoT Edge AI Autonomous Systems
