Who We Are

IETE Student Forum
at Kongu Engineering College

The IETE Student Forum at Kongu Engineering College is the official student chapter of the Institution of Electronics and Telecommunication Engineers (IETE) — India's premier professional organization for electronics, telecom, and IT professionals.

We bridge the gap between academia and industry by organizing workshops, seminars, technical talks, competitions, and industrial visits — empowering students to become industry-ready engineers.
